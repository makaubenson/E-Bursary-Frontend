SOMU Bursary Application

Ensuring student's welfare is a task assigned to the Students Organization of Maseno University(SOMU).

For that reason, the leaders decided to start a programme which would help the needy students clear their fee balances.

This program is open for all. Incase of any queries contact the SOMU Secretary via 0758413462.

Are you in need to clear your fees but you dont have any money? simply apply and attach all the relevant documents.
