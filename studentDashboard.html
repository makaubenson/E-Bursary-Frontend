SOMU Bursary Application

SOMU bursary is allocated and funded by the students body(SOMU) in assistance of the Directorate Student Affairs.

The bursary is ussualy meant to the genuinely needy students who can't raise their school fees, the disabled students and a consideration of students from manigalised counties.

After being notified, the interested students should login and apply for the bursary and submit their applications for vetting. After the vetting process, a final list of beneficiaries will be produced notifying those who got selected.

Finally, a cheque will be taken to the Students Finance department in conjunction with a list of the selected students after which the money is deposited in their individual students fee accounts.

MASENO UNIVERSITY

Maseno University is a public university based in the Maseno district of the city of Kisumu, Kenya, situated along the Equator. It was fully fledged as a university in 2001, after being a constituent college of Moi University for a decade. It is one of public universities in Kenya.
